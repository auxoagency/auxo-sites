About

Baton Rouge's corner cafe for real Italian coffee.

La Divina Italian Coffee Cafe brings authentic espresso craft to the Goodwood corridor, where a small team pulls shots, bakes fresh daily, and keeps things genuinely welcoming from the first cup to the last. This is not a chain. The recipes are real, and the room feels like it.

With a 5-star rating across 424 verified Google reviews, La Divina has quietly become one of the most trusted cafes in Baton Rouge, LA. Guests come for the cortado, stay for the pastries, and come back because the staff actually knows their name.
