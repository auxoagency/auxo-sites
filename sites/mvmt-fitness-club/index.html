About

Built for Scottsdale's Most Serious Athletes

If you're an elite fitness enthusiast who's outgrown the noise of a commercial gym, MVMT Fitness Club was built with you in mind. We operate as a private membership model - meaning the floor is never crowded, the equipment is always available, and every aspect of your program is built around your goals. Personal training and nutrition coaching aren't add-ons here; they're woven directly into what membership means.

Our Scottsdale facility runs around the clock, giving you access whenever your schedule demands it - before sunrise, after a late shift, or on a Sunday when everywhere else is closed. We pair hands-on supplement guidance with InBody body composition scans so you can track real progress, not guesswork. And when recovery matters, cryotherapy is on hand to help you come back stronger. This is comprehensive wellness under one roof, without the compromise.
