Common Questions

Answers up front.

How soon can you come out?
We often have same-day or next-day availability. Call (225) 791-9911 and we'll find the earliest slot that works for you.
Do I need to leave my home during treatment?
For most general pest treatments, you can stay home. If the scope of service requires you to vacate temporarily, we'll tell you before we start - not after.
Are your treatments safe for kids and pets?
Yes. We use EPA-registered products and follow label instructions carefully. We'll advise you on any short re-entry windows specific to the treatment applied.
What if the pests come back after treatment?
Call us. Return visits are part of what you're paying for - we don't charge extra to follow up on a treatment that needs reinforcement.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance plans?
Yes. Quarterly and monthly service programs are available for both residential and commercial customers. Ask us about scheduling options when you call.
Do you serve areas outside Baton Rouge?
We serve communities throughout the greater Baton Rouge area. See our service area section or call to confirm your location is covered.
