About

Good coffee shouldn't be hard to find in a town like Tupelo.

We've been pouring cups on North Gloster for a good while now, and we're not planning on stopping anytime soon. Strange Brew isn't trying to be some slick national chain - we're a Tupelo shop through and through. We know most of our regulars by name and by order, and that's exactly how we like it.

Whether you're ducking in before work, settling in with your laptop for a few hours, or just need somewhere to catch up with a friend, we've got you covered. Our coffee's made with care, our drinks are made to order, and our staff actually means it when they say "welcome in."
