About

When you need more than just a cup of coffee - you need a place to actually land.

Strange Brew isn't trying to be a chain. We're a Tupelo institution - the kind of spot where you recognize faces, the baristas know your order, and nobody's rushing you out the door. Whether you're catching up with a friend, getting some work done, or just need a quiet moment with something good to drink, this is the place.

We're open early and stay open late because Tupelo's got people moving at all hours. The coffee's serious, the vibe is relaxed, and the prices won't make you wince. Come see what the regulars already know.
