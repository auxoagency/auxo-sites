About

Your neighborhood spot for fresh, local finds - every morning.

If you're a Scottsdale resident looking for a morning that feels a little more alive - fresh produce, a really good cup of coffee, and baked goods worth waking up for - this is your place. We open at 6:30 AM every day of the week, which means whether you're a commuter grabbing breakfast on the go or a local building a Saturday ritual, we've got you covered before the rest of the neighborhood is moving.

We're part of the Arcadia Farms family, an established name in the Scottsdale community that's long stood for quality and care. Our marketplace brings together farm-to-table produce, curated local artisan goods, and prepared foods - all sourced and selected with the same neighborly spirit Arcadia Farms is known for. With a 4.9-star rating across 156 reviews, our guests keep coming back - and we love that.
