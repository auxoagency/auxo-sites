About

Your import's got a problem. We've seen it before.

If you drive a Honda, Toyota, Subaru, BMW, or anything else that didn't roll off an American line - you know the frustration. Dealerships charge a premium for things that don't need it, and a lot of general shops just aren't comfortable working on European or Asian imports. That's exactly why we're here in Tupelo.

We keep it straightforward: tell you what's wrong, what it costs, and get it fixed. No upselling, no runaround. We've been working on imports long enough to know where they fail and how to fix it the first time. Bring it in, and we'll take care of it.
