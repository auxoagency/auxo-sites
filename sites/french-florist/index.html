About

Greenville's go-to for blooms that mean something.

French Florist on Beverly Road has been crafting arrangements that Greenville residents reach for on their most meaningful days, from intimate table bouquets to bold statement pieces for events across the Upstate. With a 4.8-star rating across 50 Google reviews, the work speaks for itself.

Every arrangement is made by hand, using fresh-cut stems sourced to order, never sitting in a cooler for days. Walk in, call ahead, or describe what you need and the team will build around your vision, not a catalog template.
