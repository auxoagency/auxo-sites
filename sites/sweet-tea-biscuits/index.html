About

Good food shouldn't be hard to find - it should feel like coming home.

That's exactly what we set out to do when we opened up on McCullough Blvd right here in Tupelo. We didn't want another forgettable lunch spot - we wanted a place where folks could sit down, breathe, and eat something that actually tasted like somebody cared. Our biscuits are made fresh every day, our sweet tea is brewed strong, and we don't cut corners on a single thing that hits your plate.

We're only open Tuesday through Friday for the lunch rush, and that's on purpose. We'd rather do it right four days a week than stretch ourselves thin and lose what makes this place worth coming back to. If you've been looking for honest Southern food in Tupelo, we think you've found it.
