About

When you're tired of eating at your desk and craving something that actually fills you up.

We opened Sweet Tea & Biscuits because Tupelo deserved a lunch spot that doesn't feel like fast food or another chain. Everything we put on the table — the flaky biscuits, the slow-cooked vegetables, the sweet tea that's poured right — comes from recipes we actually care about. We're open Tuesday through Friday for the lunch rush, and we'd love to see you.

It's a small operation and we like it that way. You'll get a hot plate, a full glass, and a familiar face every time you walk through the door. That's what Southern hospitality's supposed to feel like, and we're not about to change it.
