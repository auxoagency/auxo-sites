About

You're tired of fast food that doesn't taste like anything - and we don't blame you.

Around here, lunch isn't just a meal - it's a break worth taking. We opened Sweet Tea & Biscuits because Tupelo deserved a place where the biscuits are made from scratch, the sweet tea's brewed strong, and the food on your plate actually tastes like somebody's grandmother made it. We keep it simple: fresh ingredients, honest portions, and no shortcuts.

We're open Tuesday through Friday from 11 to 2, which means we're not trying to be everything to everyone - we're trying to be exactly right for folks who want a proper Southern midday meal. Come hungry. Leave satisfied. That's the whole idea.
