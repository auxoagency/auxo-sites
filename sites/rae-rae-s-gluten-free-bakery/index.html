About

Billings' go-to kitchen for people who actually need gluten-free.

Rae Rae's isn't a bakery that happens to offer a gluten-free option. Everything coming out of the kitchen on N 35th St is made without gluten, full stop. That commitment has earned a 4.9-star rating from 191 Billings customers who know exactly what they're getting every time they walk through the door.

From buttery morning pastries to celebration cakes, the menu changes with what's fresh and what's in season. Whether you're newly diagnosed with celiac disease or you've been living gluten-free for years, this is the Billings bakery that finally has you covered without the cross-contamination worry.
