About

Coffee worth finding - and a place worth staying.

If you’re someone who takes your coffee seriously - or just craves a genuinely good cup and a seat that feels like home - you’ll understand what we’re building here. I opened Lost + Found because I believe an invitation for coffee is really an invitation for community. A chance to slow down, share space, and actually connect with someone. That’s the heart of everything we do.

We’re a specialty café with our own in-house roastery, launched in 2023 after years of education and experimentation. Our Head Roaster Riley oversees every bag we produce - and you can grab one right here in the shop. On the drinks side, we’ve got our rotating “Lab” menu where our baristas push the creative envelope: coffee-forward cocktail riffs, unexpected flavor combinations, and whatever’s got us excited this week. With 210+ Google reviews and a 4.5-star rating, the Tupelo community’s been kind enough to keep coming back - and that means everything to us.
