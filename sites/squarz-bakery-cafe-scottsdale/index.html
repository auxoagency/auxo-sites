About

The kind of bakery Scottsdale has been waiting for.

If you've been searching for a spot where everything coming out of the oven is worth the trip - you've found it. At Squarz Bakery & Cafe, we put real craft into every pastry, loaf, and custom cake we make. Our artisan baked goods and custom cake designs aren't produced overnight somewhere else and dropped off at the door. Everything's baked here, every morning, by people who genuinely love what they do.

Same-day baking means nothing sits around waiting. It's always fresh, always made with care - right here on 68th St.
