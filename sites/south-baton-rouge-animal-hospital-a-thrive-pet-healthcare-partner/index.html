About

Baton Rouge’s neighborhood animal hospital, backed by a national network.

South Baton Rouge Animal Hospital has been serving pets and their families from a convenient Goodwood Blvd location since joining the Thrive Pet Healthcare network. The practice offers comprehensive wellness exams, preventive care, diagnostics, and sick visits for dogs and cats throughout the greater Baton Rouge area.

With a 5-star rating across 93 verified Google reviews, the team here has built a reputation for clear communication and unhurried appointments. From annual vaccines to same-visit lab work, South Baton Rouge Animal Hospital keeps your pet's health moving forward.
