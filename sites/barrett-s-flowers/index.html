About

Greenville's neighborhood florist, right on Beverly Road.

Barrett's Flowers has been arranging fresh blooms for Greenville, SC for years, building a reputation one bouquet at a time. With a 4.8-star rating across 121 Google reviews, the shop has earned its place as one of the most trusted florists in the Upstate.

From everyday arrangements to weddings, sympathy pieces, and same-day gifts, Barrett's handles every order with the same care you'd expect from a true neighborhood shop. Stop by 11 Beverly Road any weekday from 9 AM to 5 PM, or Saturdays until 2 PM.
