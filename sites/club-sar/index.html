About

Where Every Fitness Level Finds Its Place

Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or you're a seasoned athlete chasing a new personal best, Club SAR is built around you. As a multi-sport facility with diverse fitness options under one roof, we offer fitness center memberships, group fitness classes, and personal training - giving Scottsdale residents the flexibility to build a routine that actually fits their life.

Operated by the City of Scottsdale Parks and Recreation department, we bring accessible, affordable municipal pricing to the community without cutting corners on what matters. Our doors open at 6 AM on weekdays, making it easy to get your workout in before the day takes over. With 4.9 stars across 105 reviews, the reception from the Scottsdale community has been clear - this is a space where people feel genuinely welcome, regardless of where they're starting from.
