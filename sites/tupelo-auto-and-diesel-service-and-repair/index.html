About

When your transmission's acting up, you need answers fast - not a runaround.

We've been working on vehicles right here in Tupelo for years, and we know how much a busted transmission can throw off your whole week. Whether it's a slipping gear, a check engine light you've been ignoring, or a diesel truck that just won't pull like it should, we'll tell you straight what's wrong and what it'll cost - before we touch a bolt.

We work on everything from everyday passenger cars to heavy diesel rigs. No upselling, no mystery charges. Just honest diagnosis and solid repair work from people who actually know what they're doing. Call us, come in, and let's figure it out together.
