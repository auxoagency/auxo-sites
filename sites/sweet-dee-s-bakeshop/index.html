About

For anyone who believes a really good pastry changes the whole morning.

Whether you're picking up a flaky croissant on your way through Scottsdale or planning a celebration cake that has to be exactly right, we bake everything fresh each day - from our artisan breads and pastries to fully custom decorated cakes and specialty desserts. Nothing here is made the day before.
