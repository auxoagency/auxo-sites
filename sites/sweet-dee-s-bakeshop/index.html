About

For Scottsdale neighbors who know the difference a real bakery makes.

If you're the kind of person who can tell a croissant from a crescent roll - and who believes a birthday cake should actually be memorable - we baked this place for you. At Sweet Dee's, everything that comes out of our kitchen starts with quality ingredients and ends with something worth savoring, whether that's a custom celebration cake, a morning pastry, or a loaf of bread still warm from the oven.
