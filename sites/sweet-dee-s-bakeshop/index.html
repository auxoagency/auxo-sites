About

For Scottsdale residents who want the real thing.

Whether you're stopping in for a flaky morning pastry or planning a wedding cake that needs to stop guests mid-bite, we bake everything from scratch - every single day. Our menu spans custom cakes and cupcakes, artisan breads, cookies, brownies, and whatever's beautiful and seasonal right now. Nothing gets held over. If it's in the case, it was made this morning.

We're locally operated and small by design - limited hours, limited quantities, and a 4.9-star rating built on 422 honest reviews. That's not a number we take lightly. Our Tuesday through Sunday schedule isn't a quirk; it's how we protect the time it takes to do this right. Scottsdale's had us for a while, and we're not going anywhere.
