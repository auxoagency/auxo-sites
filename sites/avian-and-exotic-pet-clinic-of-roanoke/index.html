About

Roanoke's dedicated clinic for the animals most vets don't see.

At the Avian and Exotic Pet Clinic of Roanoke, birds, reptiles, small mammals, and other exotic companions receive the focused, specialized attention their health demands. Most general-practice clinics in Virginia aren't equipped for exotic species, so our Power St SE location fills a critical gap for pet owners across the region.

Rated 4.9 stars across 78 verified Google reviews, the clinic has earned the trust of Roanoke families who keep non-traditional pets and need a provider who understands their unique physiology, behaviors, and health risks.
