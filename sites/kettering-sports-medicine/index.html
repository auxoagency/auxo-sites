Kettering's sports medicine practice that actually listens.

Kettering Sports Medicine brings focused, hands-on rehabilitation and injury care to the Hempstead Station corridor of Kettering, Ohio. Every treatment plan is built around your specific activity level, sport, and recovery goals, not a one-size protocol.
