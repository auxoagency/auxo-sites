About

Baton Rouge's most-reviewed salon on Goodwood Blvd.

Salon Raybon has built something rare on Goodwood Blvd: a full-service hair salon where stylists take the time to get it right. From precision cuts to balayage and full color, every client leaves looking intentional, not just "done." That consistency has earned a perfect 5-star rating across 283 Google reviews.

Located in Suite D at 8221 Goodwood Blvd, Salon Raybon serves Baton Rouge clients Tuesday through Saturday with skilled, attentive service and none of the rushed, transactional energy you find elsewhere. When locals want a stylist who listens, this is where they call.
