About

Billings trains harder at 35th Street.

Beartooth Performance is a results-driven training facility right on North 35th Street in Billings, MT, built for people who want more than a treadmill and a locker room. The programming is structured, the coaching is hands-on, and the 5 AM doors are open for those who mean business before the rest of the city wakes up.

Rated 4.9 stars across 82 Google reviews, Beartooth has earned the trust of Billings athletes, working professionals, and first-timers who needed a place to start strong. The facility operates six days a week, with early weekday hours designed around real schedules.
