About

Fresh blooms, made by hand, in Greenville's North Main neighborhood.

Flowerlina is a small-batch floral studio tucked on Beverly Rd, where every arrangement is cut and assembled the same morning it leaves the shop. There are no imported bulk stems sitting in cold storage here, just seasonal, intentional flowers chosen for how they look and how long they last.

Greenville customers have given Flowerlina a 4.8-star rating across 35 verified Google reviews, which reflects what regulars already know: this is the kind of flower shop where the person wrapping your bouquet knows exactly what the occasion calls for.
