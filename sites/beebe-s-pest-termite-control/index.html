Common Questions

Straight answers.

How soon can you come out?

In most cases we can schedule a visit within a day or two. Call us at (225) 924-7096 to check current availability.

Is the treatment safe for my kids and pets?

Yes. We use products approved for residential use and give you clear instructions on any brief wait times before re-entering treated areas.

Do I need to be home during the treatment?

For exterior-only treatments, no. For interior work, we ask that an adult be present so we can walk you through what was done.

What if the pests come back after treatment?

We stand behind our work. If you see activity after your scheduled treatment period, call us and we'll return at no extra charge.

Do you handle both homes and businesses?

Yes - we serve both residential and commercial customers across Baton Rouge and the surrounding area.

What pests are most common in Baton Rouge?

Louisiana heat and humidity make this prime territory for cockroaches, termites, mosquitoes, ants, and rodents. We handle all of them.
