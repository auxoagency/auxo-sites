Common Questions

What customers
usually ask.

How long does a typical treatment take?
Most residential treatments take between 30 and 60 minutes depending on the size of the home and the type of pest. We don't rush - if something needs attention, we address it on the spot.
Is it safe for my kids and pets?
We use products that are approved for residential use and will walk you through any precautions - including how long to stay off treated surfaces. Your family's safety is part of the job.
Do I need to leave during treatment?
For most general pest treatments, you can stay home. For certain applications - like indoor foggers - we'll let you know in advance so you can plan accordingly.
What if I see pests after treatment?
Some activity in the first few days after treatment is normal as pests contact the product. If you're still seeing active problems after that window, call us - we'll come back at no extra charge.
Do you offer one-time treatments or only contracts?
Both. We can do a single treatment, or set you up on a recurring schedule if you'd prefer ongoing protection. We'll recommend what actually fits your situation - not just the more expensive option.
