Common Questions

Straight answers.

Is the treatment safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use EPA-registered products and give you clear re-entry times before we start. Typically 30-60 minutes after application for interior work.

Do I need to leave my home during treatment?

For most general pest treatments, no. For certain fumigation or specialized services, we'll let you know exactly what's needed during the inspection call.

How quickly can you come out?

We serve Baton Rouge and surrounding areas and often schedule within a day or two. Call us directly at (225) 924-7096 for the fastest answer on availability.

What if the pests come back after treatment?

That's covered. We stand behind our work and return between scheduled visits if you're seeing activity that shouldn't be there.

Do you offer ongoing plans or one-time service?

Both. One-time treatments handle immediate problems. Ongoing quarterly or monthly programs keep them from coming back in the first place.

How do I know if I have termites vs. other wood damage?

It takes a trained eye - and it matters because the treatment is completely different. That's exactly what the free inspection is for.
