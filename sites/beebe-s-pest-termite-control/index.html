Rodent Removal & Exclusion

Roof rats entered the Valley from the citrus groves decades ago and never left. They nest in attics, chew wiring, and contaminate insulation. Trapping alone doesn't solve it - you have to seal the entry first.

We find every gap above the roofline, stuff and seal it, then remove the population already inside.

  • Steel mesh and foam exclusion sealing

  • Follow-up check at 2 weeks

Common Questions

Things people always ask us.

Are your treatments safe for kids and pets?
Yes. We use EPA-registered products applied at label rates. We ask that pets and children stay off treated surfaces until dry - typically 30-45 minutes. We'll give you specific guidance for each product we use before we leave.
How quickly can you get to my house?
Most jobs are scheduled within 24 hours. For bee swarms, scorpion-in-the-bedroom situations, or other urgent calls, we offer same-day slots Monday through Saturday. Call us directly for same-day requests.
Why do scorpions keep coming back even after treatment?
Sprays alone don't stop scorpions long-term because they don't groom themselves the way insects do, so contact pesticides have limited effect. Lasting control requires physical exclusion - sealing gaps - combined with regular barrier treatment. One-time sprays feel good but rarely solve it.
Do I need to be home during the treatment?
For exterior-only treatments, no - we can handle it with gate access. Interior work requires someone 18+ to be present to let us in and get sign-off before we start.
What's covered under your 30-day guarantee?
If the same pest we treated returns within 30 days, we re-treat the affected area at no charge. The guarantee applies to the specific infestation treated - not new pest species that appear after the original visit.
Do you offer monthly plans or one-time treatments?
Both. One-time treatments work well for isolated problems like a single cockroach infestation. For ongoing pests like scorpions, ants, and spiders, monthly or quarterly maintenance plans keep numbers down consistently and cost less per visit than repeated one-times.
