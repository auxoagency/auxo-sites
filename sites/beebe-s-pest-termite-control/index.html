Common Questions

Straight answers.

Is the treatment safe for kids and pets?
Yes. We use EPA-registered products and follow all label protocols. We'll let you know if any area needs to dry or air out before kids and pets return - usually just a couple of hours.
How fast can you schedule a visit?
In most cases we can schedule within 1-2 business days. For urgent situations like a termite swarm or rodent in the walls, call us directly at (225) 924-7096 and we'll do our best to get out the same day.
Do I need to be home during the treatment?
For the initial inspection and first treatment, yes - we want to walk through findings with you. For routine maintenance visits on an existing plan, we can often service the exterior without you present.
What if I see pests again after treatment?
Call us. We'll come back at no additional charge. Some treatments take a few days to fully eliminate a colony - but if you're seeing activity beyond that window, we want to know and we'll make it right.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance plans?
Yes. We offer monthly and quarterly service plans that keep your home protected year-round. Louisiana's climate means pest pressure doesn't really stop - a maintenance plan is the most cost-effective way to stay ahead of it.
