About

Springfield's gym that works on your schedule.

Gymnetix Gym at 2103 E Rockhurst St is built for people who don't keep 9-to-5 hours. The doors are open 24 hours a day, seven days a week, so early risers, night-shift workers, and everyone in between can train when it actually works for them. That commitment to access has earned Gymnetix a perfect 5-star rating across 152 Google reviews from Springfield members.

The facility is stocked for serious training without the serious attitude. Members get a clean, well-maintained space where the focus stays on the workout. Whether you're chasing strength goals, cardio milestones, or just building a consistent habit, Gymnetix Gym delivers a no-friction environment in the heart of Springfield, MO.
