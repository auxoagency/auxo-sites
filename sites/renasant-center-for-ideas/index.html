About

Starting or growing a business is hard — you don't have to figure it out alone.

Whether you've got a business idea you've been sitting on for months or you're already running a shop and can't figure out why the numbers aren't working, the Renasant Center for IDEAs exists to help. We're right here on Main Street in Tupelo, and we've worked with all kinds of businesses — from food trucks to consulting firms to makers who turned a side hustle into something real.

We don't hand you a stack of pamphlets and wish you luck. We connect you with experienced mentors, help you put together an actual business plan, and stick with you through the process. Tupelo's got a strong entrepreneurial community, and we're proud to be part of what keeps it growing.
