About

Starting a business is hard. Getting the right guidance shouldn't be.

We've been at the corner of East Main Street in Tupelo for years, and we know firsthand that the difference between a good idea and a thriving business is usually access — to mentors, to funding knowledge, to people who've done it before. That's exactly what we're here for at the Renasant Center for IDEAs.

Whether you're still scribbling on napkins or you've already hung your sign, we've got the programs, workshops, and one-on-one support to help you build something real. North Mississippi's entrepreneurial community is tighter than most people realize, and we're proud to be a part of that fabric right here in Tupelo.
