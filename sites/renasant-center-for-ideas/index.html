About Us

Starting a business is hard.
You shouldn't have to figure it out alone.

Most entrepreneurs in the Tupelo area have the drive — what they lack is a clear roadmap. At Renasant Center for IDEAs, we exist to close that gap. Whether you're writing your first business plan, seeking capital, or ready to scale, our team gives you the tools and connections to move forward with confidence.

We specialize in supporting women-owned and minority-owned businesses, startups, and established companies looking to relocate or expand in Lee County. This isn't generic advice — it's hands-on support from people who are invested in Northeast Mississippi's economic future.

Rooted in Tupelo and committed to the people who build it — that's what the Renasant Center for IDEAs is all about.
