About

Relief that goes beyond the adjustment

If you've been living with chronic pain, stiffness, or posture problems, you deserve more than a one-size-fits-all answer. At Arizona Pain & Posture, Dr. Bonacci works closely with each patient to understand what's actually driving your discomfort - then builds a care plan around it. Drawing on chiropractic care, physical therapy, massage therapy, and the Feldenkrais method, I address pain from multiple angles so you can recover more fully and stay well longer.

What sets this practice apart is the integration of modalities that rarely share a roof. The Feldenkrais method, paired with hands-on chiropractic and targeted physical therapy, gives patients in the Scottsdale area access to a genuinely holistic path to recovery. With a 4.9-star rating across 179 patient reviews, the results speak for themselves - and extended weekday hours, including evening appointments, mean care fits into your actual life.
