About

Scottsdale residents deserve care that addresses the root cause - not just the symptom.

Whether you're an athlete working through a sports injury, a parent managing back pain, or bringing in your child for their first adjustment, my approach starts with understanding exactly what's happening in your body. I'm Dr. Scott Hoffer, and alongside Dr. Brandon Hoffer, I've built this practice on one principle: every patient in Scottsdale deserves a personalized, evidence-based care plan that actually works.

Since opening in 1994, we've grown into a multi-generational practice treating infants through seniors - and everything in between.
