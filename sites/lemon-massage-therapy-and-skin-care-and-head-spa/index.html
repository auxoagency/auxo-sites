About

West Des Moines' most complete wellness studio, under one address.

Lemon Massage Therapy and Skin Care and Head Spa, located at 1000 73rd St in West Des Moines, combines three specialized disciplines in a single, calming space. Whether you're booking a deep-tissue massage, a customized facial, or a restorative head spa session, each treatment is designed around your specific needs, not a one-size menu.

With 449 Google reviews and a 4.7-star rating, Lemon has built a reputation among West Des Moines residents who want consistent, results-driven wellness care. The team is licensed, the scheduling is flexible, and the studio runs seven days a week from early morning to evening.
