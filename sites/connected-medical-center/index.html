About

Real relief, without the hard sell.

If you're dealing with back pain, a nagging injury, or the aftermath of an auto accident, I want you to feel better - not pressured. I'm Dr. Ross Dye, and at Connected Medical Center I treat Phoenix and Scottsdale patients of every age, from newborns to 90-somethings, with personalized care built around your body and your goals. I specialize in K-Laser therapy for pain management and complex auto accident and workers' compensation cases, drawing on every tool available - from chiropractic adjustments and physical therapy modalities to digital X-ray and corrective exercises - to get you back to living well.

Since opening in Scottsdale in 2016, I've built this practice on a simple promise: no scare tactics, no high-pressure long-term care plans, just honest treatment. Most insurance is welcome, including Medicare, and affordable cash prices are available for patients without coverage. If you're new here, your first visit includes a free exam, X-rays, and first adjustment - so there's nothing standing between you and getting started.
