About

For Scottsdale residents who want something genuinely made.


I'm Lior, and I bake because I love it - not because I have to fill a shelf by opening time. Every custom cake, every loaf of bread, and every specialty dessert that leaves my kitchen on East Shea is made the way it should be: by hand, in small batches, with real care for what goes into it. When you pick up an order here, you're getting something that was made for you.

My limited hours aren't an accident - they reflect the way I work. I keep things small so quality stays high, and my 4.7-star rating tells me that approach resonates. Whether you're ordering a custom cake for a celebration or just stopping in for fresh artisan bread, I want every bite to feel like it was worth coming for.
