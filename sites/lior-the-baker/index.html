About

Freshly baked, just for you.

If you're looking for baked goods that actually taste like someone made them with care, you've found the right place. I'm Lior, and I bake everything here - from whole-grain artisan loaves to custom cakes and delicate pastries - from scratch, every time the doors open. My focus is on quality over quantity, which is why I keep the menu tight and the ingredients honest.

The limited hours aren't an accident. They reflect a simple rule I hold myself to: if it's not fresh out of the oven, it's not on the counter. Scottsdale locals have responded - 221 reviews averaging 4.9 stars speaks for itself. Whether you're swinging by for a morning loaf on Friday or placing a custom wholesale order, you'll get the same level of care every single time.
