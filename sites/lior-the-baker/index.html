About

For those who know the difference a real loaf makes.

I'm Lior, and I bake for Scottsdale residents who care about what goes into their bread, their pastries, and their desserts. Every item that leaves my kitchen - whether it's a fresh loaf, a custom order, or a weekend pastry - is made by hand, from scratch, with care. That's not a tagline. It's just how I work.

My schedule is intentionally limited. Wednesday through Sunday, a few hours at a time. That's not an accident - it means every batch I make gets my full attention. With a 4.9-star rating across 221 reviews from customers right here in Scottsdale, I'll let the bread speak for itself.
