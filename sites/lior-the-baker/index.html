About

If you've been searching Scottsdale for bread that actually tastes like it was made by hand this morning - you've found it.

I'm Lior, and I started this bakery because I believe the things we eat every day deserve real craftsmanship. Every loaf of artisan bread, every custom cake, every pastry that leaves my kitchen is made to order - no shortcuts, no sitting on shelves for days. My limited hours aren't a inconvenience, they're the reason everything stays this fresh.

With a 4.9-star rating from over 221 customers right here in Scottsdale, I'm proud that word has spread the way it has - one loaf, one birthday cake, one croissant at a time. When you order from me, you're not ordering from a production line. You're getting something I made for you.
