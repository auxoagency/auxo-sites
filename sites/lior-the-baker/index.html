About

For Scottsdale residents who want something genuinely fresh.

I'm Lior, and I bake everything myself - custom baked goods, fresh breads, specialty cakes, and made-to-order pastries that come out of my kitchen on my schedule, not a factory's. That means you'll only find me Wednesday through Sunday, and every item you get was made that day.

With a 4.9-star rating from 221 customers, I'm proud that the limited hours haven't been a barrier - they're actually part of the point. When you come to me, you're getting a direct relationship with the baker, not a counter clerk. That kind of freshness and care is hard to fake, and I don't try to.
