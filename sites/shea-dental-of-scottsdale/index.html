About

Scottsdale's quiet escape - for your smile.

Shea Dental of Scottsdale has built a reputation for patient-first care in the heart of north Scottsdale, Arizona - the kind of practice where you remember the doctor's name and they remember yours. Rated 4.9 stars across 469 Google reviews, the numbers reflect something real: patients who come in nervous and leave at ease.

Located on Raintree Drive in Scottsdale, AZ 85260, the practice sees patients Monday through Thursday and keeps scheduling simple, communicative, and unhurried. Whether it's a routine cleaning or something that's been put off too long, Shea Dental approaches every appointment the same way.
